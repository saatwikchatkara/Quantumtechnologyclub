About QTC

Our Mission

The Quantum Technology Club at IIST is dedicated to advancing research and awareness in quantum computing, quantum communication, and space-related technologies. We bridge the gap between theoretical quantum mechanics and practical applications in space science.

What We Do

  • Research in quantum algorithms and protocols
  • Quantum communication systems for satellites
  • Educational workshops and seminars
  • Collaboration with industry and academia
  • Space-quantum technology integration
